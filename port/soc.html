Impact on Society

Article:

By: Karehka Ramey(useoftechnology)

Summary: technology has a substantial impact on society. as it evolves it doesn't inherently have a negative or positive outcome rather the outcome of the impact is dependent on the use we give it. for example, some positive impacts on society due to technology include mechanized agriculture for faster more efficient farming, improved Transportation and Communication for a better way of life, and an improved education system. some negative impacts on society include Resource Depletion and Increased Population. this shows that technology will always have an impact on society, but its society the determines whether or not that impact is positive.
