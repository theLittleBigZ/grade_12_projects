Future of Technology

Article:

By: Ryan Ayers(bigdata-madesimple)

Summary: AI has always been a staple of science fiction but since the discovery of neural networks and deep learning machine have been able to perform more and more complex tasks, for example, facial recognition as AI advances thanks to big data it will have a more and more significant role in our lives. firstly it will automate transportation ie. self-driving cars so you don't have to stress about driving and theoretically removing traffic for society. it will also help us create more advanced prosthetics for amputees. it can and will take over dangerous jobs to remove people from dangerous situations, become a friend/caretaker to those in need by seeming to act more human in speak and action, and even solve climate change.
